P.P.S.S.

a web platform for designing, exploring and analyzing protein-ligand structural patterns using textual notation and graphs

PPSS was my undergraduate degree project. It was born as an idea to unify two existing applications, ScanProsite for text search and GSP4PDB for graph search. PPSS unifies these two search methods into a single platform for exploring and analyzing protein-ligand structural patterns.
